What is Opsin?

Opsin is a free, web-based raster image editing application designed and created by Scott Simon.

Mission Statement

Opsin was established to provide a free, lightweight, accessible alternative to professional image editing software that often impose subscription fees and/or disruptive advertising.

Our Commitment to Uninterrupted Workflow

Opsin is 100% free to use.

  • You will NEVER be required to pay a subscription fee to use Opsin.
  • You will NEVER encounter advertisements or pop-ups while actively editing a project.

To maintain server infrastructure and cover hosting expenses, a single, brief advertisement will be displayed exclusively during the image export process.
